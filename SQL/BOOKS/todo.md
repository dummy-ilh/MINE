Based on the resources above, focus your 30-day plan on these high-yield concepts:

* **Window Functions:** `RANK`, `DENSE_RANK`, `ROW_NUMBER`, `LAG`, `LEAD`.
* **Complex Joins:** Self-joins and multi-table joins.
* **CTEs (Common Table Expressions):** Using `WITH` for cleaner, readable logic.
* **Logical Order of Operations:** Knowing that `FROM` executes before `WHERE`, and `SELECT` executes last.
