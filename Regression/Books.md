🥇 1. Regression Analysis by Example — Chatterjee & Hadi
Best all-rounder. Real datasets, worked examples, covers diagnostics, multicollinearity, transformations. Perfect interview prep balance of theory + application.

🥈 2. Applied Linear Regression Models — Kutner & Nachtsheim
Deepest theory coverage. If you want to truly understand OLS, assumptions, ANOVA, and model building — this is the one.

🥉 3. Data Analysis Using Regression and Multilevel Models — Gelman & Hill
Builds modeling intuition, not just mechanics. Sets you apart in interviews where they probe why you made modeling decisions.
